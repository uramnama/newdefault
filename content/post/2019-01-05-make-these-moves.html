Esusu is a startup company founded by Abbey Wemimo and Samir Goel. This app is designed to help people create and manage rotational savings clubs.

This app is really cool because I have first hand experience in this being a first generation Eritrean American. It all stems from communities of immigrants living in America who have leveraged small lending circles as a mechanism to save money.

“When immigrants move to America they continue to participate in this economic system, but there is no data captured or record of this system” said Goel.
